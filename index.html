Platform capabilities

The operating system for agriculture.

One platform. Eight systems. Three missions — Predict, Prove, Move — across plantations, farms and food supply chains.

01 · Intelligence

Predict.

  • AI weather forecasting

    Rainfall and harvest-readiness predictions for specific blocks and fields, not just the region.

  • AI logistics coordination

    Optimised truck routing across estates, mills, warehouses and ports — fewer empty runs, tighter windows.

  • IoT field sensors

    Temperature, humidity, soil moisture and rainfall streamed live into the forecasting models.

The outcomeKnow when to harvest. Reduce weather risk. Increase yield.
02 · Traceability

Prove.

  • Crop & tree-level inventory

    Every tree and block registered with age, yield history and health scores — your asset register, alive.

  • Image-based quality grading

    Consistent, photographic quality standards at every handoff. Disputes end where evidence begins.

  • End-to-end delivery tracking

    Transport requests, live status and confirmed receipts — the full chain of custody on record.

The outcomeEnd quality disputes. Pass audits. Command premium prices.
03 · Trade & logistics

Move.

  • Customs automation

    Export documentation and Phytosanitary Certificate processing handled inside the platform.

  • ERP integration

    Two-way sync with your existing management systems. Data is entered once, everywhere.

  • Multi-platform access

    Web dashboard for HQ, mobile app for the field, WhatsApp bot for everyone in between.

The outcomeReach markets faster. Cut logistics costs. Never stall on paperwork.
